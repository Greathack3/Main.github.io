Detective Camp

What is Detective Camp? Detective Camp is a website and soon-to-be app for anyone willing to augment their skills as a modern detective.

About-Website

Welcome, sleuth enthusiasts! This website is designed to equip you with the necessary skills to improve

your deductive abilities. Through targeted learning, AI tools, and engaging games, you can enhance your

detective skills in ways that might surprise you. My apps and games are organized into four

distinct categories, each focusing on a specific aspect of deduction.

(Observation, non-verbal-comunication, logical-thinking, Working-memory and Memory techniques)
